Mimi Kasu

Collaborating with the team, we discussed the vision for the video, focusing on how to showcase the unique designs and the atmosphere of the show while telling a compelling story that would resonate with viewers.

During the event, the electric ambiance was contagious.

I maneuvered through the venue, capturing the stunning designs on the runway and the reactions of the audience. Each moment was a chance to highlight the intricate details of the garments and the artistry of the models, creating a visual narrative that celebrated the essence of Mama Kasu.

I meticulously crafted the footage into a dynamic video that captured the spirit of the fashion show.

The final edit not only showcased the incredible designs but also conveyed the excitement and passion behind Mama Kasu. This experience deepened my love for fashion videography and reinforced the power of storytelling through visual art.
